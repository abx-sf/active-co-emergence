Research in Its Consumption

From Exposure to Protection

Part of the research sequence — Activation

Research does not begin from a decision.

It opens when something no longer works. When a form, a language, an inherited grammar cease to be sufficient.

It does not add control: it removes it.

It begins as a loss of mastery.

It is an unstable and temporary condition. It does not sustain itself

.

It may pass through a work, sometimes an entire practice, but it never definitively coincides with it. When it is declared as a stable identity, it is already over.

What opens it is not intention, but a fracture. Not biographical, not expressive. A fracture between perceptual experience and form &mdash between what is seen and what one knows how to do. Here the work does not advance: it falters. Error is not an accident, but a condition. Without this exposure, there is no research, only development.

Artistic research is a boundary space. And a boundary is not inhabited: it is crossed. If it becomes a permanent space, if it turns into a recognizable position, it ceases to be a fracture and becomes a fixed grammar. From risk it turns into method. From method it stabilizes into style. It no longer operates.

This is where research is consumed: when the term precedes the experience it is supposed to name. When the declaration replaces the loss of mastery. When continuity presents itself as discontinuity.

At that point, research is no longer exposure, but protection.

Time remains the place where it can be tested, because it is not a matter of declared urgency or formal novelty, but of non-identical repetition &mdash the persistence of a problem that does not resolve.

Research does not produce definitive outcomes, but deviations that do not close too quickly into style.

Within this framework, critical practice is not responsible for certifying research as a label. It is required to cross the same limit &mdash accepting not to know immediately how to name what is happening. Accepting the possibility of loss.

To discern here does not mean to close, but to distinguish without reducing.

In conclusion, research should not be proclaimed.

It is recognized at the point where the work stops protecting itself and accepts that it does not yet know what it is doing. There, the critical function becomes necessary again &mdash not as a position, but as a variation &mdash to prevent the fracture from closing too quickly.
